FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Where does my money go? 100% of your donations go directly to funding projects for individuals or families in need.

Can I choose who receives my donation? Donations are pooled into a collective fund, and we select beneficiaries based on their submissions and needs.

Are my donations tax-deductible? This depends on your country. Please consult a tax professional for guidance.

How do you ensure transparency? We publish updates and share impact stories regularly so you can see how your contributions are used.

About Us

The 1 Dollar Challenge was created with one simple goal: to change lives, one dollar at a time. Our mission is to bring hope, joy, and opportunities to those who need it most by pooling small donations to create a big impact.

We believe in transparency, community, and the power of kindness. Together, we can make dreams come true for individuals, families, and communities worldwide.
